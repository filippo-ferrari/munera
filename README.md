# Munera: an app to track your expenses

the idea is to manage and keep track of your expenses:
- cost
- data
- object
- category
- notes
- quantity


- have an option to setup recurring expenses (netflix, cable, etc) 
- calendar visualization (if a GUI will ever be created)
- weekly and monthly data summaries, overall and for every category or group
- insert expenses of the past and of the future
- set one or more creditor for expenses (friends that pays for you)
- see how much you own to a friend, or he owns you

interface:
either web/PWA or CLI, if ever
